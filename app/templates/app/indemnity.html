INDEMNITY BOND

IID takes the signature and left hand thumb impression on Indemnity bond from each student after course completion. Some of the important points mentioned in the bond are stated below:

  • I shall be complying to all the rules and regulations laid down by the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA), India.
  • I shall operate the Drone/ UAV/UA only for specific government requirement projects or if a requirement exists for commercial or personal Drone operation.
  • I shall take explicit permission of respective authority before I operate drones anywhere in India
  • If any mishaps occur during the operation of drones in India or abroad, Indian Institute of Drones is not responsible for my actions.
  • I completely understand that certification given by Indian Institute of Drones is only for training as per guidelines issued by DGCA and is not a licence to operate Drone in India.
  • I am responsible for all the action taken by me or my drone while I am involved in any drone operations in India.
