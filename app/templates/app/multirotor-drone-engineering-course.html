Frequently Asked Questions
How is this course beneficial for my career?

This multirotor drone engineering course will help you to enhance your technical knowledge and skills which will help you to get jobs in the drone industry.

On which kind of drone I shall get the training?

The training will be done on a basic quadcopter multirotor. The training drone will be provided by IID. However, if you want, you can bring your own drone kit as well.

Shall I be able to assemble the multirotor drone by myself after this course?

Yes, you will become an expert in assembling a multirotor drone at the end of this course. You will not only be able to assemble, but also identify and troubleshoot common problems.

Will I get to assemble the drone?

Yes! As mentioned in the curriculum, the drone assembly will be done by the instructor on the first day. On the second day, you will be assembling and configuring the drone on your own under the guidance of our instructor.

Do I have to bring my own kit for drone assembly practical?

It is not mandatory to bring your own drone kit. For training purpose, a kit will be provided by IID.

Will I also get the flying experience of drones?

No. Unless you are a certified drone pilot, you will not be allowed to fly any drone. If you want to fly your drone, then you must undergo pilot training course. You can get in touch with us to know more about the bundle course.
