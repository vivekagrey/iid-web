INSTRUCTORS

Lieutenant Commander John Livingstone

Founder, Trustee, Accountable Manager and Instructor
Indian Institute of Drones

Lt Cdr John Livingstone (Retd.) is also the Founder CEO & Product Architect of Johnnette Technologies Pvt Ltd. After working as a UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) External Pilot in the Indian Navy, he ventured into the business world with more than a decade's experience in Unmanned Systems both in the military operations and civilian applications. He is also the first Indian Naval Officer to get First Merit Award at UAV Tettra School, Indian Air Force in 2007. Under his leadership, Johnnette Technologies has been awarded with Best Technology Product Startup of the Year at Startups Entrepreneurship & Disruptive Innovation Awards 2015. He currently conducts test flights for UAVs. He is also a writer and his articles about UAVs have been highly applauded in the Unmanned Systems Industry. His book on “Life Saving Drones” is one of the highly appreciated accounts in the drone industry. Alongside being the leading light to one of the biggest names in the Unmanned Industry, he has been also coined as a fitness freak and an outdoor adventurer.

M S Prasad, Scientist [Ex - DRDO]

Chief Technical Director

M S Prasad is currently the Director of Amity Institute of Space Science and Technology, Amity University, Noida. He was a Group Director in DRDO and then had a successful entrepreneurial journey before he ventured into the academic platforms. He has decades of experience in space science and technology. He is an expert in Digital Signal Processing (image & object identification Military objects), multiple target tracking with integrated Guidance & control, Missile/Aerospace systems, Software Development and Secure data & voice network design. Amity students have been able to develop an incredible UAV under his meticulous supervision and guidance.

He was ex Additional Surveyor General before his retirement from the Indian Army. With a vast knowledge on survey, military mapping, UAV geospatial survey and much more his presence in the company has been quite instrumental in serving the defence markets.

Wg Cdr S Vijay

Visiting Faculty - Drone Technology

S Vijay is a B.Tech graduate in E&C Engg. (1993) from JNTU, Hyderabad. He did a PG certification course in Aeronautical Engineering from prestigious Air Force Technical College, Bengaluru. He is also certified on Lean Six Sigma (Green Belt) and Business Management from Administrative Staff College of India. He has held many important command & staff appointments in his 23-year service career. He has extensive experience in Operations, Aerospace Technology and Project Management. He is qualified on all variants of Surface to Air Missiles in IAF, Kiran Trainer aircraft, Electronic Warfare, Searcher Mk-II and Heron UAVs. In the year 2007, he presented a paper on collision avoidance system for UAS at World UAS Conference held in Paris. In 2010, he was deputed to NTRO under PMO as a Group Director (Engg) for induction and operationalisation of Satellite Communication UAVs for internal & external security role. He is trained on Satcom enabled Heron UAVs at IAI, Tel Aviv. Currently he is working as Vice President at Marvel Geospatial Solutions Pvt Ltd, Hyderabad looking after Operations of LiDAR, Aerial Survey & Mapping projects.
